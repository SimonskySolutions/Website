About Us

We Build What Your Business Needs to Grow

Simonsky Solutions is a software engineering firm specializing in enterprise-grade solutions for industrial businesses. We work with factories and ambitious organizations to design, build, and integrate the digital systems that eliminate friction and unlock growth.

Our flagship capability is building fully customized ERP systems for industrial factories — integrating inventory, sales, accounting, shop floor operations, and MES into a single unified platform that functions as a complete digital twin of your facility. Beyond manufacturing, we deliver cloud infrastructure, system integrations, and web applications: a full-stack technology partner built for precision.
